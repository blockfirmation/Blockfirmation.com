Our Solution: Real NFT powered by Blockchain & Web3 platform

Our first solution, Real NFT will create a reliable B2B platform for retail businesses and brands, and its customers through B2C plaform to sell or re-sell original products – authenticating genuine products that stand out from counterfeit products.

The new NFT marketplace will enable access to the Phygital NFT’s (digital twin of a product) and our unique solution will offer businesses a platform to create Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) for their physical products and publish them on the Real NFT marketplace.

The marketplace can be publicly accessed and used as a platform to validate Real NFT Phygital products and confirm authenticity. Real NFT is comprised of:

  1. Real NFT Marketplace – A new NFT marketplace for Phygital products
  2. Real NFT Mobile app – Unique mobile application to register Phygital NFTs, view your collection, transfer NFTs and access our marketplace
  3. Real NFT Business Portal - New easy to use online website for creators and manufacturers to publish their Phygital NFTs on Web3 platform

    4. Retail businesses and companies who are onboarded on to our platform will benefit from knowing their customer base and demography throughout the product lifecycle. Our solution is blockchain agnostic and predominantly applicable for premium products – however there are number of use cases we are currently exploring.
